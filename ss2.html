What do I do in this Stepping Stone?


Time Commitment:

3-6 sessions

1.

Get to know your team & figure out how you want to work together

Do these to decide how your group will function:

Setting Group Agreements (establishing a safe, brave space with one another)

Creating a Team Name (building a group identity)

Cultivating Accountability (learning to work together)

Decision Making Processes (exploring different ways to make group decisions)

Getting Stuck in a Process: Tips to Get Unstuck (understanding decision making strategies)

Best of the Best/Worst of the Worst: Team Meetings (determining what we want and don’t want in our meetings)

Make these activities part of every team meeting:

Check-In/Check-Out (developing trusting relationships and team unity)

Plus/Delta Evaluations (reflecting on what we’re doing well and what we can improve)

2.

Get oriented to the project and YPAR

Use these to begin thinking about data and mapping:

Data and Mapping Breakdown (learning how to be critical consumers and producers of data)

Data and Mapping Vocabulary Relay Race (becoming familiar with key data and mapping terms)

Do these to learn about youth participatory action research:

Youth Participatory Action Research Brainstorm (defining youth participatory action research)

YPAR Shuffle (understanding the steps in a youth participatory action research project)

Around the World Research Simulation (practicing the research process from start to finish)
