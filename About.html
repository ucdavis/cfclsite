About Us

Community Futures, Community Lore is an online resource dedicated to making culturally-rooted, youth participatory action research (YPAR) resources available for free.

To contact us, please send a message to crcinfo@ucdavis.edu.

Acknowledgements

The resources on this site originated from the work of the Intercultural Oral History Project, Community LORE, Youth In Focus, the UC Davis Center for Regional Change Putting Youth on the Map program and many, many community collaborators. Lots of people helped compile and update materials and design this online environment. For a full list of contributors click here.

This website was developed by a partnership between the Center for Regional Change and the School of Education at the University of California, Davis with generous funding from The California Endowment.
