About Us

Community Futures, Community Lore is an online resource dedicated to making culturally-rooted, youth participatory action research (YPAR) resources available for free.

Acknowledgements

The resources on this site originated from the work of Youth In Focus, the Putting Youth on the Map program at the UC Davis Center for Regional Change and many, many community collaborators. Lots of people helped compile and update materials and design this online environment.
