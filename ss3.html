What do I do
in this Stepping Stone?


Time Commitment: 3-5 sessions

1.

Reflect on your project's historical and present-day environment

Mapping Matters Skits (understanding histories of community investment/disinvestment)


The Boat Metaphor (exploring power and who has it)

2.

Explore what you already know about your community and what's important to you

Do at least 1:

Real vs. Ideal (examining your community's present and imagining its future)


Community Issue Mapping (Identifying community challenges and resources)

3.

Choose your project's focus

Choosing an Issue (deciding what you will take on as a group)
