05

Practice Research Skills



It's time to develop the skills you'll need to do your research! Stepping Stone 5 includes activities that will build your skills in facilitation, note-taking, active listening, asking questions, understanding data, and mapping.

What do I do
in this Stepping Stone?


Time Commitment: 3-6 sessions

1.

Develop core skills you'll need to do your research

For surveys and working with data, use these activities:


Tips for Conducting Surveys (practicing how to do surveys)


The Data-ing Game (generating accurate, meaningful data)



For observations, use this activity:


Show Don't Tell: Observations Practice (documenting unbiased observations)



For working with maps:


Participatory Mapping (creating your own maps with data)

For interviews and focus groups, use these activites:


Red Light Green Light: Follow-up Questions (creating and using good follow up questions)


Challenging Participants (developing facilitation skills)


Getting It Down: Note-Taking (building note-taking skills)


Outlining Thoughts: Taking Notes in Outline Form (exploring more note-taking methods)


Practice your research method and perfect your tool before data collection

Use this as a guide:


Tips for Testing your Research Tool (trying out and fine-tuning what you designed)

2.

Ready to get started?
